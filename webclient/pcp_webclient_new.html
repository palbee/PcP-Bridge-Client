      Workflows edit your video before it's published. This means that the video you upload will have a college-specific intro video added to the beginning, as well as a title overlay and a CMU watermark. Then the server will encode and publish your file to a web server. Finally, you'll receive an email with a link to your video.

      Select any of the General Use Workflows if you do not have a custom workflow for your course. If you would like a custom built workflow for your course, simply fill out the form on the right side of this page.

Guidelines

    1. Files larger than 500MB should be submitted from a lab on campus.
    2. Files must have one of the following extensions: MP4, MOV, M4V, AIF, AAC.
    3. Videos are processed on a first-come, first-served basis, so your submission will not begin encoding until jobs previously submitted are complete.
    4. During heavy-usage times, submissions may take up to 24hrs to process
    5. Users will receive a notification email whenever a submission finishes processing.
    6. For a real-time view of what's processing, see below:

Following upload, your submission will be listed on the image above along with a green progress bar. You will also see a colored indicator on the left side of your submission title.

  • A green indicator means that the server is processing the submission.
  • A blue indicator means that the submission has finished processing and the user has been sent a notification email.
  • A red indicator means that the submitted content failed to process.
      • If you notice a red indicator, please verify that your file ends with the appropriate extension (mp4, mov, m4v, aif, or aac) and resubmit your content. If that does not resolve the issue, please email the "Upload ID" below to coffm1pc@cmich.edu or contact the CMU Media Production office at 989-774-6640.
